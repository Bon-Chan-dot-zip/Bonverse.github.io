Outer Gods

Outer gods are beings of immense power, there is a hierarchy, with some being simply really powerful beings to beyond multiversal concepts. But generally, those that carry this title simply reside in multiple or higher spatial dimensions, or realms, and have some power over reality. Because of their nature, mortals tend to have an extremely hard time when meeting or seeing their true forms, typically resulting in madness or death. To be a follower of an outer god you quite literally need their permission to worship them, if you dont get it, you will be entirely incapable of doing any kind of act of worship, as the will of said god will prevent you from doing so. Most gods also have a domain (Yeah I know like jjk, shut up I got this idea from Fate reality marbles), basically an extra-dimensional plane of existence solely reserved for that god and permitted people

Queen of Bronze

The Queen of Bronze is the outer god of metal and machines. Her domain is a vast field of golden wheat, scattered about are unknown and large bronze machines. Lying in ruin but occasionally one can hear a small ticking sound from them.
